Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with Friendly Party Rental

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for summer weekends and holidays. Popular items like tents and bounce houses book up quickly. However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests when inventory is available.
Do you deliver and set up the equipment? +
Yes! We offer full delivery, setup, and teardown services. Our professional crew will arrive before your event to set everything up and return at the end to break it all down. This service is included or available as an add-on depending on your rental package.
What is your service area? +
We primarily serve Syracuse, Onondaga County, and the surrounding Central New York area including Oswego, Auburn, Fulton, Baldwinsville, Liverpool, Fayetteville, Manlius, and more. Contact us to confirm delivery to your specific location.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Online bookings can be completed securely through our Booqable booking portal. A deposit is typically required to hold your reservation.
What happens if it rains on my event day? +
We understand weather is unpredictable! Our tent rentals are perfect for rainy-day protection. For other equipment, we work with you on a case-by-case basis. Contact us as soon as possible if weather is a concern, and we'll do our best to accommodate rescheduling or modifications.
Can I add items to my order after booking? +
Yes, you can typically add items to your order as long as they are available and you contact us with sufficient notice. We'll do our best to accommodate additions to your rental package.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations made 14+ days before your event receive a full refund of any deposit. Cancellations 7-13 days before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days may forfeit the deposit. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us and we'll work with you.
Is the equipment clean and sanitized? +
Absolutely! All of our equipment is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before and after every rental. We inspect each item for quality and safety before delivery.
Do I need to be home for delivery? +
For most deliveries, especially tents and larger equipment, someone should be available to confirm the setup location and ensure everything meets your expectations. For smaller items, we may be able to arrange unattended delivery — just contact us to discuss.
Are bounce houses safe? +
Yes! Our bounce houses meet all safety standards and are regularly inspected. We provide setup instructions and safety guidelines. Adult supervision is always recommended when children are using bounce houses.
