Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for most rentals, 4-8 weeks for large tents and wedding packages.

Do you deliver and pick up?

Yes! We offer delivery and pickup throughout the Syracuse area. Delivery fees vary based on distance from our Minoa warehouse.

What areas do you serve?

All of Central New York including Syracuse, Minoa, Cicero, Manlius, North Syracuse, Liverpool, Fayetteville, Camillus, Baldwinsville, Clay, Cazenovia, and surrounding communities.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before your event receive a full refund. Please call us to discuss your situation.

Do you set up tents?

Yes, our crew handles all tent installation and breakdown. Setup service for tables and chairs is available for an additional fee.

Can I book and pay online?

Yes! Browse our inventory and book with secure Stripe payment right on our website. Available 24/7.
