Our Story

Friendly Party Rental was founded with a simple mission: to make event planning stress-free for families, businesses, and couples throughout the greater Syracuse area. We believe every celebration deserves quality equipment and professional service.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into one of Central New York's most trusted rental companies, serving thousands of events each year — from backyard birthday parties to grand wedding receptions.

We take pride in our equipment quality, on-time delivery, and friendly customer service. When you book with Friendly Party Rental, you're getting a partner committed to making your event perfect.
