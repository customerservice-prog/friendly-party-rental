About Friendly Party Rental

Family-owned and operated in Minoa, NY — proudly serving Central New York for over 10 years.

Your Trusted Local Party Rental Company

Friendly Party Rental has been serving the Syracuse, NY area for over 10 years. We started as a small family operation and have grown to become one of the most trusted names in party and event rentals across Central New York.

Based in Minoa, NY, we deliver throughout Onondaga County and surrounding areas. Our team is dedicated to making your event a success with clean equipment, on-time delivery, and friendly professional service.

Whether you need tables and chairs for a backyard party, a tent for a wedding, or bounce houses for a kids party, we have the inventory and experience to make it happen.
