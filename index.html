Syracuse NY Party Rentals - Tents, Tables, Chairs, Bounce Houses & More

Friendly Party Rental has been the trusted name in party rentals across the Syracuse, NY area for over 10 years. As a family-owned and operated business based in Minoa, we deliver high-quality event equipment throughout Central New York including Cicero, Manlius, North Syracuse, Liverpool, Fayetteville, and Camillus.

Tent Rentals in Syracuse, NY

Our tent rental inventory includes pole tents, frame tents, and high-peak tents in sizes from intimate 10x10 canopies to 40x80 wedding tents that seat 300+ guests. Every tent is professionally cleaned, inspected, and installed by our experienced crew.

Table and Chair Rentals

We stock thousands of tables and chairs for delivery across Onondaga County. Choose from 6-foot and 8-foot banquet tables, 60-inch round tables, white folding chairs, resin padded chairs, and chiavari chairs in multiple colors.

Bounce House Rentals

We have a huge selection of inflatables including castle bouncers, combo bounce-and-slide units, waterslides, obstacle courses, and interactive games. Every inflatable is professionally cleaned and safety-inspected before every rental.
